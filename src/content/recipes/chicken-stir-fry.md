## Instructions

1. Cook rice according to package directions.
2. Slice chicken into thin strips. Cut broccoli into florets and bell pepper into strips.
3. Heat sesame oil in a wok or large skillet over high heat.
4. Cook chicken until golden, about 4 minutes. Remove and set aside.
5. Add broccoli and bell pepper. Stir-fry for 3 minutes.
6. Return chicken to the wok. Add soy sauce, grated ginger, and minced garlic.
7. Toss everything together for 1 minute until the sauce coats evenly.
8. Serve over rice.
